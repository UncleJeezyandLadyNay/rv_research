# Outdoors RV Titanium Series - Maintenance Product List
*BackCountry 26RPS · BackCountry 28DBS · Blackstone 280RKS · Timber Ridge 28BKS*
*Date: 2026-05-22*

Products chosen specifically for ORV construction — fiberglass exterior, MorRyde suspension, Onan generator, Lamilux panels, and rubber-seal-heavy Titanium package. Different from the Alliance list in several key areas.

## Generator Maintenance (26RPS & 28DBS — Onan 3.6k LP)

| Product | Use | Where to Buy | ~Cost |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Onan A070G876 Spark Plug (2-pack)** or equivalent Champion RJ19LM | Annual spark plug replacement — verify gap at .020" before installation. This is the documented factory-error plug | Amazon, NAPA, Camping World | ~$10-15/pair |
| **Onan 122-0836 Air Filter** | Annual air filter replacement for 3.6k LP Onan | Amazon, Camping World, RV dealers | ~$15-25 |
| **Briggs & Stratton SAE 30 Motor Oil** | Oil change medium if generator has run 100+ hours | Auto parts, Walmart, Amazon | ~$8/qt |
| **Star Tron Enzyme Fuel Treatment** | Fuel stabilizer for end-of-season generator storage — treats LP fuel system | Amazon, Camping World | ~$12 |
| **Cummins Onan Generator Service Kit** (when available for 3.6k model) | Annual service in one box | Cummins dealer, Amazon | ~$30-50 |

### Notes
- The Onan 3.6k LP generator in both the 26RPS and 28DBS has the same spec spark plug — Champion RJ19LM is the most commonly stocked cross-reference at hardware stores
- Always verify the gap (.020") before installing any replacement plug — the documented factory error was a wrong gap, not wrong plug. A correct plug with wrong gap causes the same rough-running issue
- Log generator hours at every maintenance event — Onan recommends service at 150-hour intervals

## Stabilizer Jack Replacement
*Budget item — do before first trip*

| Product | Use | Where to Buy | ~Cost |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Lippert Trailaire Equalizer Hitch** (if not already running WDH) | Not a stabilizer replacement but addresses tongue-weight stability at the hitch end — relevant for 28DBS | Camping World, Amazon | ~$300-500 |
| **Camco Olympian Heavy Duty Stabilizer Jacks** (pair) | Direct replacement for undersized stock jacks | Amazon, Camping World | ~$80-120/pair |
| **BAL Accu-Slide Stabilizer System** | Scissor-style replacement stabilizers with better leverage | Amazon, Camping World | ~$150-200/pair |
| **X-Chock Wheel Stabilizers** (pair) | Between-wheel chock system that dramatically reduces trailer rock — supplement or replace scissor jacks | Amazon, Camping World | ~$60-80/pair |

### Notes
- X-Chock wheel stabilizers are the highest-value immediate upgrade — install them between the wheels on the same axle and most of the side-to-side rock disappears. Fast field install, no tools
- For a permanent solution, replace the scissor jacks with Olympian or BAL units — they have more mechanical advantage
