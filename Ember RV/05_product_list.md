# Ember RV — Maintenance Product List
*Overland 221MSL · E-Series 26ETS · Touring Edition 2500RDL*
*Date: 2026-05-23*

Products chosen for Ember's specific construction: Azdel Onboard composite walls, Tufflex PVC roof (E-Series/Touring), EmberTrack E-Track hardware, CURT Touring Coil suspension (2500RDL), and Ground Control TT3 auto-leveling (2500RDL).

---

## Sealants
*Required annually regardless of roof or wall material*

| Product | Use | Where to Buy | ~Cost |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Dicor 501LSW Self-Leveling Lap Sealant** (white) | All horizontal roof surfaces: A/C bases, vent rims, MaxxAir fans, antenna mounts, solar brackets | Amazon, Camping World | ~$12/tube |
| **Dicor 505LSW Non-Sag Lap Sealant** (white) | All vertical: sidewall seams, corner caps, window trim, front cap seam | Amazon, Camping World | ~$12/tube |
| **Dicor RP-RC-1 Rubber Roof Cleaner** | Pre-cleaning before re-sealing on the Overland (TPO roof) | Amazon, Camping World | ~$15 |
| **Eternabond RoofSeal Tape** (4" white) | Emergency field repair — bonds aggressively to both Tufflex PVC and TPO | Amazon, Camping World | ~$25/roll |
| **Geocel Pro Flex Crystal Clear** | Sealing around the Stargazer skylight perimeter (221MSL) — flexible, UV-resistant, bonds to mixed materials | Amazon, Home Depot | ~$12/tube |

### Notes
- Buy 2 tubes of 501LSW and 2 tubes of 505LSW per season
- **221MSL Stargazer skylight:** keep 1 tube of Geocel Pro Flex specifically for the skylight seal — it bonds to the skylight frame-to-roof interface better than Dicor in some applications
- Keep 1 tube of each sealant in the trailer at all times for field repairs
- The Tufflex PVC roof on the 26ETS and Touring Edition is more UV-resistant than TPO but the Dicor sealant at all penetrations still degrades — do not skip annual inspection

## Roof Care
*Tufflex PVC (E-Series/Touring) and TPO (Overland) — different products*

### Tufflex PVC Roof — E-Series 26ETS and Touring Edition 2500RDL

| Product | Use | Where to Buy | ~Cost |
|---|---|---|---|
| **303 Aerospace Protectant** (large spray) | UV protection for Tufflex PVC membrane — apply after cleaning, annually | Amazon, auto parts | ~$15 |
| **Mild dish soap + soft brush** | Annual wash of Tufflex PVC surface — gentle clean, no petroleum solvents | Anywhere | ~$5 |
| **Dicor RP-RC-1 Rubber Roof Cleaner** | Can also be used on Tufflex; removes road grime at penetrations before re-sealing | Amazon, Camping World | ~$15 |

### TPO Roof — Overland 221MSL

| Product | Use | Where to Buy | ~Cost |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Dicor RP-RC-1 Rubber Roof Cleaner** | Annual wash of TPO membrane | Amazon, Camping World | ~$15 |
| **Dicor RP-CRP-1 Rubber Roof Conditioner** | UV protection coat for TPO after cleaning | Amazon, Camping World | ~$15 |

### Notes
- Do NOT use petroleum-based solvents, citrus degreasers, or abrasive pads on either Tufflex or TPO — both will deteriorate under petroleum exposure
- The Tufflex material carries a manufacturer's lifetime warranty on the material itself, but that does not cover sealant failures at penetrations — those are maintenance items
