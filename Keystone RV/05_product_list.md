# Keystone RV — Maintenance Product List
*Cougar Western Elevation 28BHWE · Cougar Half-Ton 28BHS · Cougar Half-Ton 29RLP · Outback 252RD*
*Date: 2026-05-23*

Products chosen for Keystone's specific construction: one-piece TPO rubber roof, HyperCore composite walls, HyperDeck inorganic flooring, Tru-fit slides, SolarFlex solar system (252RD), and BAL Norco 5.1 stabilization (252RD).

## Sealants
*Non-negotiable — Keystone's warranty excludes water damage from "inadequate seal maintenance"*

| Product | Use | Where to Buy | ~Cost |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Dicor 501LSW Self-Leveling Lap Sealant** (white) | All horizontal roof surfaces: A/C bases, vent collars, solar panel mounts (252RD), antenna, all four roof edge seams | Amazon, Camping World | ~$12/tube |
| **Dicor 505LSW Non-Sag Lap Sealant** (white) | Vertical: all sidewall seams, corner caps, window trim, front cap seam | Amazon, Camping World | ~$12/tube |
| **Dicor RP-RC-1 Rubber Roof Cleaner** | Annual clean of TPO roof before re-sealing — removes road grime and degraded sealant | Amazon, Camping World | ~$15 |
| **Dicor RP-CRP-1 Rubber Roof Conditioner** | UV protection for TPO membrane — apply after cleaning annually | Amazon, Camping World | ~$15 |
| **Eternabond RoofSeal Tape** (4" white) | Emergency field repair for any roof breach — bonds aggressively to TPO | Amazon, Camping World | ~$25/roll |

### Notes
- Buy 2 tubes 501LSW and 2 tubes 505LSW per season — use them both
- **Keystone warranty protection:** Keep the empty tubes and the date you used them. They are physical proof of maintenance if a warranty claim is disputed.
- Keep 1 tube of each in the trailer for field repairs
- The front and rear wall-to-roof seams are the documented highest-failure spots on Keystone roofs — prioritize these in spring inspection

## SolarFlex System — Outback 252RD

| Product | Use | Where to Buy | ~Cost |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Dicor 501LSW** (also listed above) | Re-sealing solar panel mount points — these are roof penetrations | Amazon, Camping World | ~$12/tube |
| **Soft microfiber cloth + mild dish soap** | Annual solar panel cleaning — removes bird droppings and debris reducing panel output | Anywhere | ~$5 |
| **NOCO GENIUS2 Battery Maintainer** | Winter battery maintenance while SolarFlex provides passive solar charge — prevents sulfation | Amazon | ~$35 |
| **Digital Multimeter** | Verify MPPT controller output and battery bank health | Amazon, Home Depot | ~$30–50 |

### Notes
- SolarFlex Protect 220 (standard) uses a 200W rooftop panel. The MPPT controller display should show positive charging current on any sunny day — verify this at least once per season.
- If upgrading to SolarFlex Discover 440i with Dragonfly Energy lithium batteries: lithium batteries require a compatible smart charger and do not need float maintenance like AGM/lead-acid. Confirm your shore power charger is lithium-compatible before connecting.
