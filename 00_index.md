### Alliance Quick Reference

**5 Documented Factory Defects (verify at PDI):**
1. **281BH solar wiring** - leads to single 6V battery instead of series. Test: ~44VDC at MPPT PV terminals on sunny day
2. **GFCI wiring** - washer/dryer outlets wired incorrectly at factory. Test: trip and reset, verify correct outlets respond
3. **Shower door seal** - leaks around and under door. Test: 3 minutes running, check floor outside pan
4. **Drain valve handles not connected** - pull each handle, verify actual valve moves at tank
5. **Azdel mounting failures** - push on every wall-mounted component before signing

**Warranty trap:** Alliance excludes water damage from "inadequate seal maintenance" even when a factory defect caused the breach. Photograph every exterior seal before leaving the lot.

### Outdoors RV Quick Reference

**5 Documented Factory Defects (verify at PDI):**
1. **Stabilizer jacks inadequate** - universal across all ORV models. Budget $300-500 for replacement before first trip
2. **Water fill line clamps wrong size** - blow off on first connection. Connect city water slowly, walk system under pressure
3. **Back flush valve backwards** - documented on 28DBS. Run tank flush, verify flow direction
4. **Generator spark plug gap** - factory ships .028" instead of spec .020" on 26RPS / 28DBS. Pull a plug, verify gap before leaving lot
5. **Auto leveling calibration** - Lippert Ground Control TT may need re-zeroing. Have dealer run full cycle at delivery
